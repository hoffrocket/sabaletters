Saba Letters

My grandparents emigrated to Palestine in the mid thirties from different cities in Poland. They met there and started a life together in a new land. Between 1935 and 1941 they received letters from their families in Europe, and from each other. This archive of letters is dedicated to their memory.

- Jon Hoffman
