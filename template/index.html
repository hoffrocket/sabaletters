Saba Letters

My grandparents immigrated to Palestine in the mid thirties from different cities in Poland. They met there and started a life together in a new land. Between 1935 and 1941 they received letters from their families in Europe, and from each other. This archive of letters is dedicated to their memory.

Each line on the grid above represents a full year and each point along a line is a letter written in that year. Click on the points to read the letters.

- Jon Hoffman
